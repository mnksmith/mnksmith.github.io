About

Matt NK Smith is a lifelong fiddler, at home across bluegrass, old-time, Irish trad, jazz, and classical music. He's enjoyed performing on stages around the world, from orchestral sounds in Carnegie Hall to singalongs in the pubs of Geneva, from kitchens in Alaskan villages to Kentucky bluegrass festivals. These days he's based in Burlington, Vermont, singing folk songs for his two young kids and chipping away at a solo project.
