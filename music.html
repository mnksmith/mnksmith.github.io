About

Matthew N.K. Smith is a fiddler and violinist whose playing draws from old-time, folk, bluegrass, jazz, Irish traditional, and classical music. A lifelong string player, he has performed across the US and Europe. By day he works as a data scientist at Spotify; before that he was Principal Data Scientist for the City of Boston and worked in ocean conservation at Oceana. He holds a PhD in particle physics from Columbia University.
